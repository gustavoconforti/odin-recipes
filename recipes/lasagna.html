Lasagna

Description

This is an easy to cook homemade lasagna recipe.

Ingredients

  • 6 lasagna noodles
  • 1kg of beef stew
  • 500g of mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1/2 bellpepper
  • 1/2 tomato
  • 1/2 can of tomato sauce
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Add 2 to 4 tablespoons of olive oil in a medium to large pan, followed by all of the chopped vegetable. Wait for them to lose color.
  2. Add the beef stew little by little, alongside with appropriate amounts of water, making sure all chunks of meat are separated. The beef should be covered by no more than 1cm of water in the end, let it cook and fully dry.
  3. Remove the excess of oil from the cooked meat.
  4. Add the tomato sauce, water and salt. The liquid should not be too watery nor consistent.
  5. Assemble the lasagna. The layers, from bottom to top, should read: lasagna noodles, meat, cheese. Repeat until there are no more ingredients.
  6. Take to the oven for 15 minutes, at 170 celsius degrees.
  7. Eat!
